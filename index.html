Space Explorer!

  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game
  • There are four space icons below. By clicking on a space icon you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  • You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose if your total score goes above the random number.
  • The value of each space icon is hidden from you, but you can discover it by clicking it.
  • Each time the game starts, the game will change the value of each space icon.
